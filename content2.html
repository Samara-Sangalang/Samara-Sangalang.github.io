------In the midst of the global pandemic, it is truly disheartening and concerning to see students, teachers, and staff suffering great amounts this pandemic due to the strain imparted by the Philippine Science High School System to all of its components. In class, all scholars do in both synchronous and asynchronous meetings are to go in to twitter to complain, grumble, and whine about their struggles. I personally find it very concerning, the fact that these scholars suffer this way. It annoys me that the system does nothing, but most importantly, I am infuriated that scholars take in to social media platforms calling for reform disregarding the formal ways of communication between the admins and the student body.
