It is certainly not an everyday occurrence that you see the number one trending hashtag in twitter happens to be ‘#PisayGiveUsABreak.’ Our scholars nowadays waste their time going out in social media, ranting on twitter, talking to friends, eating, and sleeping instead of doing their requirements. Objectively speaking, as future scientists of our generation, Philippine Science High School Scholars must know that prioritizing requirements is key instead of whining, crying, and putting effort on superficial things like numeric grades.
