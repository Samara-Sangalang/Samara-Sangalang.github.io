It has been unanimously agreed upon the scholars that indeed, this workload and this strain exerted upon them nowadays is too hard, coupled with the fact that we are in a global pandemic. This is all they say while basking on the comfort of their soft, cushioned seats, typing tweets in the most expensive, high-end laptops available to this time. They have no other responsibility but to study, so why are they still complaining? They do nothing but to complain of inadequate time allotments, and ‘unrealistic’ goals set by Philippine Science High School module writers and reviewers. I do not know about the scholars, but for me, twenty to thirty requirements to accomplish a week seems like a breeze. They could do four to six requirements per day, and it only takes thirty minutes to do each. One minute on the introduction, seventeen minutes on the module material, and twelve minutes making a fully-fledged poster with top-notch design skills, good quotes, and APA citation on references.
