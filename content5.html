In order to solve this plaguing problem, I wish to propose a solution specifically tailored for our spoiled scholars- it may sound counterintuitive, but it admirably works nonetheless for accomplishing workloads like theirs. It is incredibly simple, if they cannot find time to do requirements, they should not sleep, eat, or socialize until all workload is done. Their mental and physical well-being could wait for their requirements, as their academic requirements are much more important than bodily requirements such as that. If there is a factor that prohibits the scholar from doing their requirements on time, they should know better to do them ahead of time- they are Pisay Scholars after all! What is the purpose of being a childhood prodigy and a genius if they cannot even tell the possible future, and do their requirements under a short period of time?
