I propose that such students that cannot keep up with the system be disposed of, any student that finds this workload mentally and emotionally taxing is nothing but detrimental to the Philippine Science High School community. I suggest sending them out of the school, but before so, I suggest charging them hefty fines for not complying in a system they are already out of. As for our remaining scholars, I suggest adding even more requirements in their workload. Through some research by browsing the #PisayGiveUsABreak hashtag on Twitter, I have concluded that the assigned requirements that we give to our scholars is not enough, as they are not learning at all. Learning takes a lot of practice, therefore increasing student workload might help with the comprehension of our students. Philippine Science High School Students would catch up flawlessly, as they have the mental capacity to cope, after all.
