Overall grades by batch would increase, now that the bad seeds are weeded out of this place. Now, if this to you sounds inhumane, the only thing I can say is, it is not. Once again, they are Philippine Science High School Scholars. They could adjust, they could adapt, and they could overcome. The system doesn’t need a change, as it was tried and tested for such a long time already. A change in the system would radicalize online learning and by extension, even physical learning as a whole might be changed. Making this any easier would just make it harder for everyone to readjust again.
