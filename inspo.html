what inspired me?


my motivation for this topic is the #PisayGiveUsABreak hashtag that trended in October 19, 2020. a lot of DDS trolls online connected the academic break agenda with a conspiracy... calling us dilawans... due to this, some of my batchmates and friends were harassed via twitter dms. so, i thought: what if i wrote in the perspective of said people? i find it quite funny and heartbreaking that people would go through those lengths just to harass 9th graders. so, i did. this whole essay was written under a persona completely out of the philippine science highschool system, and i made them as close-minded and as ignorant as possible.
